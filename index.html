Yansong Tang

I am currently a Sponsored Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, working with Prof. Philip H. S. Torr. My research interests lie in computer vision. Currently, I am working towards understanding human behaviors from various types of videos, including instructional videos, sport videos, egocentric videos and RGB/depth/skeletal videos.

Prior to that, I received my Ph.D degree in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu, and B.S. degree in 2015 from Tsinghua University. My Ph.D dissertation was awarded Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award of Tsinghua University at 2016.
